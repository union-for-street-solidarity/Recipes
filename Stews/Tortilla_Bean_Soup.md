# Tortilla Bean Soup

This is a really yummy vegetarian (if you use vegetable broth isntead of chicken broth) soup thats served over crumbled chips and cheese. it melts together and softens the chips so anyone can eat it :)

- Yield: ~6 Gallons
- Serves: ~60 people
